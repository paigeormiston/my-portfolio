Able
Concept · Branding · Mobile UX/UI
PriestmanGoode proposed a design challenge to "Design a mobile product and/or service that improves the public transport experience for people with disabilities that will, in turn, improve the experience for all." My solution is Able - a mobile application that allows persons with disabilities to access on-demand public transportation from their iPhone. All concepts, designs and animations were created by me using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects.
