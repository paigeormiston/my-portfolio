UNSPO
Concept · Branding · Mobile UX/UI
This app was created by me and Evan Donnelly as a solution to the dreadful feeling in everyone's daily routine that they have absolutely nothing to wear. Unspo uses your closet to find outfits inspired by clothes you already have. Users can purchase clothes they like, favorite and browse their looks, and view statistics about the clothes they currently have in their closet or outfits they have favorited.
